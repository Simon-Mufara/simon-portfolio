About Me

Aspiring bioinformatician passionate about solving Africa's most pressing health challenges through data-driven innovation

Background

With a background in Health System Sciences from the University of the Witwatersrand and current Honours in Bioinformatics from the University of Cape Town, I specialize in applying computational tools to improve disease understanding, diagnostics, and treatment across diverse populations.
