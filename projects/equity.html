Global Health Equity Program

Healthcare Access Programs & Data Analytics across Sub-Saharan Africa

Project Period: January 2024 - December 2024
Organization: Johnson & Johnson Innovative Medicine, Midrand
Role: Global Health Equity Intern

Overview

Led strategic initiatives and data analytics for healthcare access programs across Sub-Saharan Africa, working directly with executive leadership to improve healthcare outcomes for underserved populations. Developed comprehensive dashboards and analytical frameworks that informed policy decisions affecting approximately 10,000 patients.

Key Achievements

  • Presented strategic vision and progress reports directly to CEO and senior leadership
  • Secured executive sponsorship and resources for program sustainability
  • Supported 3 major healthcare access programs across Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Contributed to policy recommendations impacting ~10,000 patients
  • Developed 5+ comprehensive dashboards and reports
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams of 10+ staff members
  • Integrated data insights into strategic planning processes
  • Improved healthcare management through data-driven decision making

Technology & Tools

Power BI Looker Studio SQL Excel Python Data Analysis

Program Areas

1. Healthcare Access Initiatives

Supported three major programs focused on improving healthcare accessibility:

  • Treatment access programs for chronic diseases
  • Diagnostic capacity building initiatives
  • Healthcare infrastructure improvement projects
  • Community health worker training and support

2. Data Analytics & Reporting

Developed comprehensive analytical frameworks to track and improve program performance:

  • Real-time Dashboards: Created interactive dashboards tracking key performance indicators across all programs
  • Patient Outcome Metrics: Developed systems to monitor treatment outcomes and adherence rates
  • Resource Utilization: Analyzed resource allocation efficiency and identified optimization opportunities
  • Geographic Analysis: Mapped healthcare access gaps and program reach across regions
  • Impact Assessment: Quantified program impact on patient outcomes and healthcare system improvements

3. Executive Communication

Regular engagement with C-suite and senior leadership:

  • Quarterly strategy presentations to CEO and executive team
  • Monthly progress reports highlighting key metrics and insights
  • Ad-hoc analyses for strategic decision-making
  • Business case development for program expansion
  • Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

Dashboard Development

Program Performance Dashboard

  • Patient enrollment and retention trends
  • Treatment adherence rates by region
  • Healthcare provider engagement metrics
  • Supply chain and logistics tracking
  • Cost-effectiveness analysis

Patient Outcomes Dashboard

  • Clinical outcomes tracking
  • Quality of life improvements
  • Adverse event monitoring
  • Treatment success rates
  • Comparative effectiveness analysis

Stakeholder Engagement Dashboard

  • Partnership metrics and engagement levels

  • NGO partnership effectiveness

Executive sponsorship secured for long-term program sustainability
